# Strategic Alignment Report: M-25-22 and USDA's IVN

**Date:** April 28, 2026

## 1. Executive Summary

This report details the strategic alignment between the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Memorandum M-25-22, "Driving Efficient Acquisition of Artificial Intelligence in Government," and the USDA's Internal Vision for the Nation (IVN) database. Our analysis reveals significant synergies, particularly concerning **risk management, compliance, and system authorization**.

The key takeaway is that USDA's existing governance frameworks and data sources, as cataloged in the IVN, provide a strong foundation for meeting the AI acquisition requirements mandated by M-25-22. By leveraging these existing assets, USDA can ensure a compliant, secure, and efficient AI adoption process.

This document groups the most significant alignments by their relevant M-25-22 components and concludes with a consolidated set of actionable recommendations for leadership to capitalize on these findings.

## 2. Alignments by M-25-22 Component

The following sections detail the specific alignments found between M-25-22 requirements and existing USDA and Federal guidance within the IVN database.

### Component: 4.d.iii.D - OMB Memorandum M-25-21 Compliance Requirements

**M-25-22 Requirement:** Contracts must ensure compliance with minimum risk management practices for high-impact use cases as required under M-25-21.

**Analysis:** This requirement for robust risk management in AI contracts is directly supported by several foundational OMB circulars and NIST frameworks present in our IVN. These documents establish the government-wide standards for risk management, information security, and privacy upon which our AI acquisition strategies can be built.

**Key Alignments:**

| --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------- |

| OMB M-21-02, Fiscal Year 2020-2021 Guidance on Federal Information Security and Privacy Management Requirements               | OMB                                               | 3               |
| NIST AI 100-1, Artificial Intelligence Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)                                                 | NIST                                              | 2               |

### Component: 4.e.i - Authorization To Operate (ATO) Compliance

**M-25-22 Requirement:** Any AI systems and services operated as an information system by or on behalf of an agency must receive an Authorization to Operate (ATO).

**Analysis:** The ATO requirement is a cornerstone of Federal IT security. The IVN database contains numerous NIST Special Publications that define the Risk Management Framework (RMF) and the specific security controls necessary to achieve an ATO. This indicates that our processes for AI system authorization can be integrated into our existing, well-defined cybersecurity compliance workflows.

## 3. Consolidated Recommendations

To leverage these strategic alignments, we propose the following actionable recommendations for leadership:

| Recommendation ID | Recommendation                                                                                                                                                           | M-25-22 Component(s) Addressed |
| :---------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------- |
| REC-01            | **Integrate AI Risk into ERM:** Formally integrate the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) into the agency's existing Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) processes as defined by OMB Circular A-123. | 4.d.iii.D                      |
| REC-02            | **Update ATO Procedures for AI:** Direct the OCIO to update the agency's Authorization to Operate (ATO) procedures to include specific controls and considerations for AI systems, referencing NIST SP 800-37 and SP 800-53. | 4.e.i                          |
| REC-03            | **Develop AI-Specific Contract Language:** Create standardized contract clauses for AI acquisitions that explicitly require compliance with M-25-21 and reference the risk management practices found in the aligned OMB memoranda. | 4.d.iii.D                      |
| REC-04            | **Mandate Cross-Functional Teams:** For all AI procurements, mandate the formation of cross-functional teams including representatives from acquisition, legal, privacy, and cybersecurity to ensure all facets of risk and compliance are addressed from the outset. | 3.a, 4.a                       |
