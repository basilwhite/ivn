### Recommendations
Based on the alignments, we recommend the following actions:
- **Management:** For each aligned component, establish a working group to define how the IVN asset can be leveraged to meet the M-25-22 requirement. This includes defining metrics and tracking progress.
- **Communication:** Develop a communication plan to showcase how existing IVN assets demonstrate compliance with M-25-22. This can be used in reports to OMB and other stakeholders.
