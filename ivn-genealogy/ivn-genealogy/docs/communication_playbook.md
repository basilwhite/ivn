# Governance Communication Playbook

## Goal
Communicate IVN-based recommendations in a way that leadership can act on and defend.

## Audience Modes
- Executive mode: mission impact, risk, urgency, decision ask.
- Oversight mode: traceability, statutory alignment, legal defensibility.
- Implementation mode: owners, sequencing, dependencies, metrics.

## Standard Narrative Flow
1. What decision is needed now.
2. What evidence supports the recommendation.
3. What authority chain is affected.
4. What changes and why this is the best option.
5. What risks exist and how they are mitigated.
6. What action is requested and by when.

## Message Discipline
- Use evidence-first statements.
- Distinguish facts from interpretation.
- Always include confidence score and source trace summary.
- Include no-change baseline for credibility.

## Briefing Outputs
- One-page decision brief for senior leaders.
- Evidence annex with alignment IDs and row snapshots.
- Implementation timeline with owners.

## Quality Checks Before Release
- Recommendation can be traced to specific alignments.
- Confidence and uncertainty language are explicit.
- Legal and oversight considerations are acknowledged.
- Action request is specific, time-bounded, and assigned.
