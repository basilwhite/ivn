# Evidence-Based Governance Proposal Protocol

## Purpose
Use IVN alignments to produce decision-grade recommendations for governance change.

## Required Sections
1. Decision Question
2. Evidence Graph
3. Authority Chain
4. Change Hypothesis
5. Impact Analysis
6. Alternatives
7. Confidence and Source Trace
8. Implementation Path

## Authority Chain Coverage
Every package should explicitly map to the applicable layers:
- Policy
- Program
- Regulation
- Executive Order
- Public Law

## Confidence Standard
- High: 0.80 to 1.00
- Medium: 0.50 to 0.79
- Low: 0.00 to 0.49

## Decision-Readiness Checks
A proposal is decision-ready only if:
- At least two alternatives are present, including no-change baseline.
- Source trace includes concrete evidence references.
- Confidence score and confidence label are present and consistent.
- Facts are clearly separated from analysis.

## Escalation Guidance
- Policy and program updates: medium evidence threshold.
- Regulatory recommendations: high evidence threshold with legal risk note.
- Executive Order and Public Law recommendations: highest threshold with explicit statutory conflict or gap mapping.
